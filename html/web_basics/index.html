Aliens live among us!

I love aliens, they are fun to be around because they don't follow many social norms. Sometimes they take people for rides in their space ships which is cool because traveling the universe is interesting. The truth is out there!

  • Introduce yourself
  • Play checkers (Aliens REALLY like checkers!)
